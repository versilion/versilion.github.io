About me

Welcome! I'm an ambitious electrical engineering student currently pursuing my degree at the University of Missouri, with sights set on graduation in 2026.
In the realm of creativity, I find my solace in video editing, particularly excelling in Adobe After Effects, a skill I've honed over four dedicated years.
For many years, I planned on pursuing a career in the visual effects industry, but I’ve decided to pursue it as a hobby instead.
Beyond the technicalities of circuits and semiconductors, I immerse myself in crafting personal video projects during my spare time, designed to ignite a spark of inspiration in others and encourage them to embark on their own video editing journeys.
This fusion of engineering prowess and artistic passion is a core part of who I am, driving me to not only succeed in my academic pursuits but also to share the joy of video creation with the world.

"The worst enemy to creativity is self-doubt.”
