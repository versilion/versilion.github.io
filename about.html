About me

Welcome! I'm an ambitious electrical engineering student currently pursuing my degree at the
University of Missouri, with sights set on graduation in 2026. In the realm of creativity, I find my
solace in video editing, particularly excelling in Adobe After Effects, a skill I've honed over four
dedicated years. For many years, I planned on pursuing a career in the visual effects industry, but
I’ve decided to pursue it as a hobby instead. Beyond the technicalities of circuits and semiconductors,
I immerse myself in crafting personal video projects during my spare time, designed to ignite a spark
of inspiration in others and encourage them to embark on their own video editing journeys.
This fusion of engineering prowess and artistic passion is a core part of who I am, driving me to not
only succeed in my academic pursuits but also to share the joy of video creation with the world.

Education

I graduated from Grain Valley High School with a High School Diploma, and afterwards, I pursued
my passion for automotive technology by attending an automotive tech school where I earned a certificate
in automotive technology. Building on this foundation, I am currently enrolled at the University of Missouri
with plans to complete mybachelors in electrical and computer engineering and graduate in 2026.
This educational journey has been a progression from high school, through specialized training
in automotive technology, to pursuing a higher education degree,allowing me to combine my technical
skills and academic knowledge for a well-rounded education.
